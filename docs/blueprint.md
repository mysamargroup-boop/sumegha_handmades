# **App Name**: Sumegha Handmades

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog & Search: Browse a curated collection of handmade art products with search and category filtering options on attractive home and listing pages.
- Detailed Product View: View comprehensive product details including high-resolution images, descriptions, pricing, and variant options on dedicated product pages.
- Wishlist Management: Users can add beloved handmade items to a personal wishlist for future reference and purchase, persisted in the database.
- Shopping Cart Functionality: Add, remove, and manage quantities of products in a persistent shopping cart.
- WhatsApp Order Checkout: A modal or form to facilitate order placement directly via WhatsApp, submitting cart contents to the business.
- Product Sharing: Integrate a share button on product pages to allow users to easily share products across various social platforms.
- AI-Enhanced Product Discovery: Provide a tool to suggest products to users based on items they have viewed or popular categories within handmade arts.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary brand color: A deep, rich raspberry tone (#C71D85), evoking creativity, passion, and elegance. This serves as a warm anchor for the 'feminine' aspect of the palette.
- Background color: A very light, desaturated rose-pink (#FBEFF6) providing a soft and clean canvas that enhances the vibrancy of products without distraction, subtly harmonizing with the primary hue.
- Accent color: A vibrant yet sophisticated orchid-purple (#D966D9), used for call-to-action buttons, interactive elements, and key highlights, providing a harmonious but distinct contrast to the primary.
- Gradient elements: Incorporate gradients using the primary, background, and accent colors for various UI elements and backgrounds, especially on attractive section backgrounds and the customized scrollbar, to fulfill the request for 'gradient feminism colour' and 'element background colour design'.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' (serif) for an elegant, fashionable, and artistic high-contrast look suitable for titles and featured text.
- Body font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for clean, modern, and highly readable body text, ensuring clear product descriptions and interface elements.
- Utilize modern, clean line icons for cart, wishlist, share, and navigation elements. Emphasize a delicate yet distinct visual style fitting for handmade arts.
- Mobile Navigation and Footer: Implement a 'proper mobile nav footer design' for intuitive access to categories, account, and business information, ensuring a responsive and appealing experience across all devices.
- Home Page Design: Create an 'attractive and modern features home page' with clear product showcases, featured items, and easy navigation to product categories.
- Scrollbar Design: Implement a custom thin-width scrollbar with a subtle gradient color, as requested, ensuring no arrows are displayed.
- Subtle UI Transitions: Employ smooth, unobtrusive animations for state changes (e.g., adding to cart/wishlist) to enhance user experience without feeling distracting.
